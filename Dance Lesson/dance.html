The main benefits of dancing for health

1. improves agility and flexibility
2. improves balance and coordination
3. improves cardiovascular health
4. improves muscle tone and strength
5. maintains and builds bone strength
6. aids in weight loss or maintenance
7. improves memory and cognition
8. improves mood
9. reduces stress levels
10. provides an opportunity to socialize
11. boosts self-esteem
